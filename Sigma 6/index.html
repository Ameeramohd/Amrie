Parrot

  • Parrots are colorful, intelligent birds known for mimicking sounds and human speech. They have strong curved beaks, zygodactyl feet, and love social interaction.
    Many live long lives and enjoy tropical environments.Parrots are renowned for their intelligence and ability to mimic sounds, including human speech. They also have strong beaks and are known for their vibrant colors and social behavior.

Lovebirds

  • Lovebirds are tiny parrots known for their affectionate nature and bonding with their mate. They come in various colors and are highly social and playful. Although small, they’re energetic and curious, requiring attention and interaction. They thrive in pairs and love snuggling together, earning them the name “lovebird.”

Lutino lovebirds

  • Lutino lovebirds are a vibrant mutation of the peach-faced lovebird. Their yellow bodies and red eyes make them stand out. These small parrots are energetic, affectionate, and form strong pair bonds. They enjoy toys, flying around, and socializing with their owners. They're ideal for bird lovers who want a loyal pet.

Coctiel

  • Cockatiels are gentle, sociable parrots with a signature head crest and orange cheek patches. Native to Australia, they love whistling and mimicking sounds. These birds are friendly, easy to tame, and make great companions. They enjoy head scratches, perching on shoulders, and bonding with their human caretakers.
